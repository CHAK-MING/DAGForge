# DAGForge Airflow Benchmark Stability Report

## Scope

This report covers the Airflow benchmark family generated by `bench/scripts/gen_airflow_bench_dags.py`. The runner config is `bench/airflow_bench.toml`.

Throughput is reported as **tasks/s** and is calculated as `total_tasks / wall_seconds`. **All-runs TPS** is the mean throughput across all 3 runs, while **Warm TPS** is the mean throughput of runs 2 and 3.

## What The Metrics Mean

- **Mean lag (s)** is total scheduling wait time across all measured tasks.
- **Stdev / CV** show run-to-run spread. CV normalizes spread against the mean.
- **Wall mean (s)** is the average wall-clock time of one run, excluding daemon startup.
- **All-runs TPS** is mean completed-task throughput across all runs in tasks/s.
- **Warm TPS** is mean throughput of runs 2 and 3.
- **p95 / p99 lag** show tail scheduling delay.
- **Root avg lag / downstream avg lag** split entry tasks from later tasks.
- **Makespan efficiency** is ideal critical-path time divided by actual end-to-end time.
- **Amplification** is the inverse of makespan efficiency.
- **Warm mean lag / Warm CV** summarize lag after the first run.
